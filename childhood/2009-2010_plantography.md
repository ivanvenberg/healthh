# Plantography (Foot Imprint Analysis) — 2009 & 2010

Ink foot imprints taken as part of the annual dispensary examinations to assess arch height and flat foot grade.  
**Source:** Scanned imprint sheets (pg. 2 bottom and pg. 7 of scan)

---

## October 2009 (Age ~11)

**Date:** 10/15/2009  
**Result:** Both feet labeled **"I"** — Grade I flat feet (bilateral)

- Right foot: broad midfoot contact area consistent with grade I flat foot
- Left foot: same

**Interpretation:** Grade I (mild) — longitudinal arch slightly reduced, no functional impairment.

---

## September 2010 (Age ~12)

**Date:** 09/15/2010  
**Result:** Both feet labeled **"I"** — Grade I flat feet (bilateral)

- Right foot: similar imprint to 2009
- Left foot: same

**Interpretation:** Grade I flat feet unchanged over one year. Consistent with orthopedist findings in both the 2009 and 2010 dispensary checkups.

---

## Notes
- Grade I flat feet are mild and common in children; often corrects with age or targeted exercises.
- Orthopedist recommended arch support insoles (longitudinal and transverse) and swimming.
- No progression noted between 2009 and 2010.
