# Note — Macro Restriction Experiment, May 2026

**Date:** ~May 2026  
**Context:** ~1 year into antidepressant treatment; currently on Wellbutrin 300mg; mood stabilizing; gym resumed in April.

---

## What happened

Set calorie/macro restrictions as an attempt to manage weight. Lasted a couple of days.

**Result:** Mood crashed hard. Two days of not understanding why everything felt awful — then connected it to the food restrictions.

**Decision:** Abandoned the approach. Chose stability over the deficit.

---

## The realization

> "My body literally falls into depression from food restrictions."  
> "I'd rather be fat but alive than thin and dead."  
> "Felt like a weight lifted off my shoulders."

---

## Why this matters

- Caloric restriction is a real neurological stressor — affects neurotransmitter availability, energy, motivation
- For a brain still recalibrating after ~1 year of medication changes, this is not trivial
- Mood stability is the priority right now — weight is secondary
- This is not weakness. This is reading data correctly and acting on it.

---

## What's working instead

- Gym 3×/week (resumed April 2026) ✓
- Not actively restricting — just not adding stress
- Wellbutrin 300mg — positive energy shift noted

---

*Saved to remember: the restriction approach doesn't work for this body at this time. The experiment ran. The result was clear.*
