## Assessment

**Contributing factors:**
1. Shift in food preferences toward fatty foods, likely related to psychopharmacotherapy (possible effect on histamine receptors/appetite)

**Associated risks:**

## Treatment Plan

### 1. Pharmacological Weight Correction

**Semaglutide (Ozempic/Wegovy)** — GLP-1 agonist

**Titration schedule:**
- **0.25 mg** SC once/week × 4 weeks
- **0.5 mg** SC once/week × next 4 weeks
- **1.0 mg** SC once/week thereafter
  - (In Europe, possible escalation to 1.7 mg → 2.4 mg for Wegovy; availability may be limited in Belgrade)

**Alternative if semaglutide unavailable:** Liraglutide (daily SC injections; effective but ~10% less effective for weight loss)

**Logistics:**
- Prescription: needs full name (in English), date of birth
- Purchase: Aim for Ozempic/Wegovy 1 mg; if sales refused, seek alternative suppliers
- Photo of medication after purchase to document start date

> ⚠️ **Outcome note (from patient):** Ozempic was stopped — patient turned out to be in the ~1% of non-responders for whom it doesn't work.

### 2. Dietary Recommendations

- **Protein:** Increase to **1.3–1.5 g/kg body weight/day** (to preserve muscle mass during therapy)
- **Fats:** Limit to reduce risk of nausea/vomiting/diarrhea (e.g., limit sauces like pesto)
- **Meals:** Continue main meals

### 3. Physical Activity

- **Strength training:** Continue 3×/week
- **Cardio:** Introduce regular moderate cardio (as tolerated)
- **Daily activity:** Increase (walking)

### 4. Alcohol

- **Risk information:** Pancreatitis risk when combining alcohol and GLP-1 agonists
- **Recommendation:** Use "conditionally safe" dosages/frequency (separate memo provided); ideally minimize/exclude during therapy start

### 5. Smoking

- **Recommendation:** Reduce/quit; discussion with psychiatrist about cessation strategy deferred (decided: not now)

### 6. Supportive Medications for GI Side Effects

| Symptom | Medication |
|---------|-----------|
| Constipation | Forlax (polyethylene glycol) |
| Nausea | Ondansetron |
| Diarrhea | Loperamide |
| Spastic pain/discomfort | Mebeverine or Trimedat |
